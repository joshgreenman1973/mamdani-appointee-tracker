How this list is built

  • Who is included. Mayoral and agency political appointees in the civil-service exempt class — deputy mayors, commissioners, agency heads, chiefs, senior advisors, press and policy staff, and board appointees. Rank-and-file civil servants are excluded by design.
  • Salaries. A green dollar figure is shown only where the city publishes an official base salary for that title (the Quadrennial Advisory Commission's mayoral-appointee schedule). Everyone else is marked "not yet disclosed" until they appear in the Citywide Payroll dataset, which lags by fiscal year. No salary is estimated or invented.
  • Dates. Reflect the announcement or confirmation date where reported; "transition" marks hires made during the December 2025 transition before Inauguration Day.
  • Sources. Each entry links to a primary or news source. Seeded from the Mayor's Office press releases, City & State New York, FOX 5 and the New York Building Congress administration tracker.
  • Status. "Retained" marks officials kept on from the Adams administration rather than newly brought in. The departed column includes Adams-era holdovers who left as the administration changed.

This is a working draft, not an official roster. Corrections and additions welcome.
