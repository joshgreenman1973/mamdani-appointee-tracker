# FOIL request — full Mamdani-administration appointee roster

**Why this is the unlock.** The mid-level political layer (deputy and assistant
commissioners, agency chiefs of staff, general counsels, executive directors,
senior mayoral advisors, policy and intergovernmental staff) is not published in
press releases, is absent from the Green Book as machine data, and will not appear
in the Citywide Payroll dataset until fiscal year 2026 posts later this year. The
Mayor's Office of Appointments maintains the authoritative internal list. A FOIL
request is the way to get the whole layer — with titles, salaries and start dates
— now rather than waiting on the payroll lag.

**Where to file:** NYC Mayor's Office, via the OpenRecords portal
(a860-openrecords.nyc.gov). Optionally file a parallel request to the Department
of Citywide Administrative Services (DCAS), which administers personnel records.

---

## Draft text

To the Records Access Officer, Office of the Mayor:

Under the New York Freedom of Information Law (Public Officers Law, Article 6,
sections 84-90), I request records sufficient to identify all non-civil-service
(exempt, non-competitive and unclassified) appointments made across the Office of
the Mayor and city agencies from January 1, 2026 to the present.

Specifically, I request a list — preferably as a spreadsheet or other machine-
readable export — containing, for each such appointee:

1. Full name;
2. Title;
3. Agency or office;
4. Civil-service class (exempt, non-competitive, unclassified, or other);
5. Appointment or start date;
6. Annual base salary;
7. Where applicable, the date of separation and the name of any successor, for
   appointees in these classes who have left since January 1, 2026.

This request is limited to appointed and exempt positions. It is not a request for
records on rank-and-file competitive-class civil servants (for example, police
officers, firefighters, sanitation workers or teachers).

I request the records in electronic form. If any portion is withheld, please cite
the specific statutory exemption and release all reasonably segregable portions.
If fees are expected to exceed $25, please notify me before incurring them.

I am a journalist and this request is made in the public interest; I ask that any
applicable fees be waived. Please acknowledge receipt within five business days as
required by law.

[Name / outlet / contact]
